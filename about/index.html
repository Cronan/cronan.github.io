I'm a Principal Engineer and Head of Solutions Technology at Man Group in London. I lead the engineering function that powers Man Solutions' bespoke, multi-asset investment mandates - portfolio optimisation, mandate design, scenario analysis, client reporting - and the tools that help Solutions win new business.

My remit is to make that work at scale, with clean architectures and tight collaboration between engineering, quant, investment, and client-facing teams.

What I focus on

  • AI deployment in regulated environments - designing systems where AI output can be observed, reversed, and enforced
  • Engineering leadership - building teams that compound capability over years, not just ship features
  • Risk and governance in technology - how to absorb errors cheaply, from any source, human or machine
  • Portfolio construction and optimisation technology - the intersection of quant research and engineering

What I do

  • Lead a team of software engineers delivering high-reliability systems for portfolio construction and analytics
  • Own the technical strategy for Solutions Technology - architecture, tooling, standards, and delivery models
  • Partner with quants and PMs to build optimisation frameworks used across mandates
  • Build analytics and modelling tools that support sales pitches and new business
  • Set engineering standards, CI/CD pipelines, observability, and long-term platform design

Background

I grew up in Durban, South Africa, where I started programming as a boy on a Sinclair ZX81 - 1KB of RAM, a membrane keyboard, and BASIC. That was 1981. I've been writing code ever since.

Before Man Group, I spent 25 years in quantitative analysis and financial technology:

At Man Group I also spent several years in risk technology, building front-office risk systems and reporting. Combined with my earlier work on the quant team at GLG Partners, I have built systems across three domains that are usually siloed: quantitative research, risk, and client solutions.

  • Brevan Howard - front-office development, software architecture, team leadership
  • GLG Partners - senior member of the quant team, quantitative analysis and development
  • Merrill Lynch - financial technology
  • Barclays Capital - front-office systems

I've worked mostly in C++, C#, Python, and distributed systems, with a particular interest in mathematics, portfolio construction, benchmarking, and optimisation.

What I'm focused on now

AI in production engineering - not the hype, but the reality of integrating AI coding assistants and agentic systems into teams that build financial software. The challenge isn't capability; it's designing systems and workflows where AI output can be trusted.
