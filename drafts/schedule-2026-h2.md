## Essay pipeline (pick per month; menu, not sequence)

Structural-variety notes assume the current ten: openings already used include
pattern-observation, anecdote-scene, authority-claim, personal-anecdote,
reference (HN thread; arXiv paper), direct question, byte-cost mid-thought,
git-artefact. Endings already used include aphorism (x2), warning, injunction,
backlog-prescription, open questions, "check me".

1. **The Mills essay** - trust-not-capability translated for retail financial
   services AI, from the FCA submission. Opens with a regulatory scene or a
   number from the Call for Input. The policy frame is the Haldane move and
   the site has never done it.
2. **The leash** - how much scope does one clean agent session earn? Graduated
   autonomy, blast radius, and why "it worked last time" is not a policy.
   Extends The surrender note's ending into practice. The natural sequel while
   the session is fresh.
3. **Gates that scale with stakes** - what pass-rate gates miss when agentic
   changes touch money, clients, or regulatory filings. Moves Propose, not
   promote from the lab to the desk. Concrete gate-failure opening.
4. **Two emulators** - differential testing as the cheapest trust primitive
   there is. Technical, code-forward, HN-friendly; the zx81 repo supplies the
   worked example. No essay currently opens with code.
5. **The junior year** - a follow-up to Building engineers (May 2025) with a
   year of observed evidence, anonymised. Needs internal data; only write it
   when the evidence is real. (The compromised-witness bar applies to this one
   with full force.)
6. **What the export month meant** - capability controlled at the border:
   governments now treat model weights as dual-use. Topical and perishable;
   write in July or not at all.

Standing rules: read `writing/index.html` and the two most recent essays
before drafting; run the humaniser corpus checks after; the disclosure line
established in The surrender note is the precedent for any essay drafted with
model assistance; the six-file grep checklist on publish.
