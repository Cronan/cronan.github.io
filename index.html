I lead Solutions Technology at Man Group, managing the engineering function that powers Man Solutions' bespoke, multi-asset investment mandates — portfolio optimisation, mandate design, scenario analysis, and client reporting — and the tools that support new-business efforts.

Before Man Group, I spent 25 years in front-office technology across Brevan Howard, GLG Partners, Merrill Lynch, and Barclays Capital, building systems in C++, C#, and Python.

Right now I'm focused on how AI — from coding assistants to agentic systems — is reshaping how we build and think about financial software. The bottleneck isn't capability. It's trust. AI is only a force multiplier if the underlying system is designed to absorb mistakes cheaply.

I've been writing code since 1981, on a Sinclair ZX81 in Durban, South Africa.
